About the Game

The short, honest version:

Spy Rider is what happens when off-road dirt racing meets spy missions.
You take a bike, hit rough hill climbs, dodge obstacles, and get thrown into quick stealth sequences where one bad move sends you back down the slope.

No long cutscenes.

No endless tutorials.

Just: engine on → track ahead → try not to wipe out.

The missions mix fast racing, off-road chaos, and those small “oh no” moments when you nearly miss a jump but somehow land it anyway. Sometimes you push forward at full throttle.
Sometimes you slow down, scan the terrain, and pick your line carefully — because speed alone won’t save you.

It’s messy in the good way!

FEATURES THAT MAKE IT WORTH PLAYING

  • Real hill climbs

    Steep, nasty, unpredictable terrain. You’ll feel every bounce.

  • Off-road physics that aren’t fake

    If you misjudge a landing — you’ll know.

  • Spy missions woven into races

    Avoid guards, grab intel, escape before they catch up.

  • Chase sequences

    Cars behind you, obstacles in front of you, brain somewhere in the middle.

  • Pure speed mode

    No enemies, no stealth. Just racing.

  • Off-road + ATV-inspired track design

    Bigger jumps, rougher curves, terrain that feels alive.

  • Works offline

    Signal optional. The engine always starts.
